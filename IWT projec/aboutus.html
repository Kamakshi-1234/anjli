Space + Parallax + Investors: Discover our strategy

For innovative entrepreneurs and passionate investors, we are building the bridge between private capital and the emerging space companies.
We have a deep network of private investors that trust the management team's allocation of capital into strategically important space companies.
Our Vision is to Build out a Strategic Value Chain:

We began building our expertise in Earth Observation (EO) and are now early stage investors in EO start-ups that are developing solutions for agriculture, insurance, and energy.


Our recent investment in an emerging European launch company comes after years of studying the market trends; Launch is evolving and innovative companies are always of interest to us, from niche satellite launchers to space planes.


The CisLunar and NewSpace Economy is a technologically vibrant market, developing, launching and maintaining next generation satellites and space craft; some focussed mainly on servicing. The next step is to build out space stations, orbiting terminals, and space based solar power, in key orbits.


We focus on strategic locations on the Lunar surface that can be explored, the resources extracted, and ultimately processed into propulsion, and commodities for energy and infrastructure.

What We Do

• Developing, supporting and funding space-focussed businesses.
• Investing in, incubating, and co-founding innovative space companies.
• SVI uses its extensive network to bring individuals and companies together to execute highly technical business plans.

We are global. We operate from Frankfurt, London and Paris.

How We Do It

• Smart acquisitions: SVI leverages European and US space networks to find the best positioned start-ups within their respective market.
• Adding value: SVI uses its in-house proprietary research and networks to add value as a shareholder and develop strategic partnerships.
• Research is at the core of what we do: we have over 10 years of experience in researching emerging space opportunities in technology and business ventures.
• Advocacy: SVI is also an advocate for lobbying governments to increase their budget allocation into the space resources sector.
